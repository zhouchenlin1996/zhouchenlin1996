About me

Hello, welcome to my website! My name is Zhou Chenlin. I am currently a Master student at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering from Tongji University, Shanghai, China.

My current research interests mainly focus on the incorporation of state-of-the-art machine learning techniques into robotic perception and manipulation.

Interests

  • 3D Vision
  • Robotic Perception
  • Robotic Manipulation
  • Machine Learning
